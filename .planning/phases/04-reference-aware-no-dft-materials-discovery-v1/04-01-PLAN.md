# Phase 4 Plan 01: Assemble Two-System Reference Packs And Benchmark Configs

## Objective

- **What:** Add the explicit input-side reference-pack layer and commit the
  benchmark configs that turn `Al-Cu-Fe` and `Sc-Zn` into Phase 4 benchmark
  lanes.
- **Why:** Phase 3 proved source-backed ingest works, but the repo still lacks a
  reusable multi-source reference-pack artifact and committed richer configs for
  the two benchmark systems.
- **Output:** Deterministic reference-pack assembly/reuse, two committed
  benchmark configs, and focused tests proving the richer inputs are
  reproducible.
