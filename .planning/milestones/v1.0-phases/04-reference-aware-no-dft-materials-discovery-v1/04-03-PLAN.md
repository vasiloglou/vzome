- **What:** Turn the new Phase 4 benchmark lanes into an operator-usable

- **Why:** Phase 4 is the moment where "reference-aware no-DFT discovery" needs
  to become a real benchmarkable workflow rather than a collection of stronger
  internals.

  that the two-system benchmark matrix works and yields comparable artifacts.
